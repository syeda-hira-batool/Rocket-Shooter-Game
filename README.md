#  Space Shooter — Ugutna War

![Gameplay Screenshot](image(1).png)

A **story-driven 2D space shooter** built using **C++ and Raylib**.

In the year **3157**, the alien **Ugutna Empire** conquered Earth and enslaved humanity. You are the only survivor who escaped with the last fighter ship. Fight through enemy fleets, collect upgrades, and defeat **Emperor Ugutna** to save humanity.

---

## Features

-  Smooth arcade-style space shooter gameplay  
-  Story mode with intro and ending scenes  
-  Multiple enemy waves with increasing difficulty  
-  Multi-phase boss battle (Emperor Ugutna)  
-  Power-ups: Double Shot, Triple Shot, Rapid Fire, Shield, Extra Life  
-  Explosion effects and animated backgrounds  
-  Procedural sound effects + background music  
-  Score tracking and level progression  

---

##  Tech Stack

- **C++**
- **Raylib**
- STL (`vector`, `string`, `cmath`)

---

##  Controls

| Action | Keys |
|--------|------|
| Move | WASD / Arrow Keys |
| Shoot | SPACE / Z |
| Continue | ENTER |

### Mission
Survive. Upgrade your ship. Defeat Emperor Ugutna. Free humanity.
